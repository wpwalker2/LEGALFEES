What We Do

My Legal Fees, LLC was created for the purpose of providing legal firms and their clients more flexibility when establishing an agreement on the repayment of services being rendered.  The process of billing and collections is often made far more difficult than it needs to be, which is where we come in to fill the gap. We assist in establishing, monitoring, and in providing transparency into any agreed upon payment plan. Our main goal is to provide both legal firms and their clients with a user-friendly experience that makes an already stressful process a bit less burdensome.
